## Tip Examples (3 out of 15)

### Tip #1: Invest in a Quality Microphone

```
Description:
Your microphone is the single most important factor in dictation accuracy.
A cheap USB mic ($15) will add background noise and miss consonants. A decent
headset ($40-80) with noise-canceling technology makes a dramatic difference.

Pro Tip:
USB headsets with cardioid patterns (like a Plantronics or Audio-Technica)
reduce background noise by 50-70%. Condenser mics capture too much ambience—
stick with dynamic mics that focus on your voice.
```

### Tip #4: Speak at a Moderate, Consistent Pace

```
Description:
Rushing or slurring creates gaps in transcription. Speaking too slowly (under
100 WPM) isn't natural and can introduce artifacts. Aim for 120-140 WPM—the
speed of normal conversation. Consistency matters more than speed.

Pro Tip:
Instead of: 'I'llmeetwithjohnabout...' try: 'I will meet with john about the
Q3 report next Tuesday.' Let each word breathe naturally.
```

### Tip #13: Build a Personal Vocabulary List for Your Domain

```
Description:
Add custom terms to Whisper Cheap's settings. Legal terms (pro bono, deposition),
medical jargon (myocardial, thrombosis), developer slang (refactoring, deployment).
Pre-training the AI on your vocabulary reduces errors by 10-15%.

Pro Tip:
If you're a lawyer, add: 'habeas corpus', 'amicus curiae', 'pro bono'. Whisper
Cheap will learn these terms and transcribe them accurately. Build this list
over time as you encounter repeated terminology.
```

## FAQ Section (3 out of 6)

### Q1: How long does it take to get proficient with voice typing?

```
Most people reach comfortable proficiency (80+ WPM, 95%+ accuracy) within 1-2
weeks of regular use. Peak efficiency comes around week 3-4. The learning curve
is much shallower than traditional typing, which takes months to master.
```

### Q3: What if I have an accent? Will it still work?

```
Yes. Whisper Cheap uses Parakeet V3, trained on 99 languages and accents.
It's very tolerant of regional accents, speech impediments, and non-native
speakers. Test with 30 seconds of your natural voice to see how it performs.
```

### Q5: How much does background noise affect accuracy?

```
Every 10dB of noise adds roughly 2-3% error rate. At 40dB (quiet office),
expect 98%+ accuracy. At 70dB (loud coffee shop), expect 90-92% accuracy.
For critical work, dictate in quiet environments. For drafts, noise is acceptable.
```
